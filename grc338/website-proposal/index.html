About

Oceans in the Sky Press is an independent publisher based in California. We pride ourselves in beautiful books with unique stories, and take the time to create each as a work of art.

As authors ourselves, we understand the complexities of the publishing industry and work with authors to create the best possible process from submission to publication date. With high royalties and some of the best distribution in the industry, Oceans in the Sky Press will bring your book to life and stand with you every step of the way!
